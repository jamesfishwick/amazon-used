Cheapest Read — Privacy Policy

Effective 2026-04-19. Last reviewed 2026-04-19.

Summary: Cheapest Read does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal data. All scan results stay on your device.

What Cheapest Read does

Cheapest Read is a Chrome extension that scans your Amazon wishlist and surfaces the lowest total price (item price plus shipping) across new and used offers for each book on the list, so you can buy the most affordable copy without manually checking every listing.

Data we collect

None. The extension does not collect, transmit, log, or share any personal data, browsing history, authentication credentials, financial information, location, or web activity beyond what is required to operate the wishlist scan locally on your own device.

Where scan results are stored

All scan results are stored exclusively in your browser via Chrome's chrome.storage.local API. Data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any server controlled by the extension author or any third party. You can clear stored results at any time by removing the extension or clearing extension storage in Chrome.

Third-party services

None. The extension does not integrate any third-party analytics, telemetry, error reporting, advertising, or remote-config services. The only network requests the extension makes are to Amazon endpoints your browser is already authenticated against — specifically, your own wishlist page and the per-book offer-listing pages you would otherwise have to open manually.

Permissions

  • activeTab — read the Amazon wishlist tab you are actively viewing.
  • scripting — inject content scripts that parse the wishlist and per-book offer pages.
  • tabs — navigate to and read offer-listing pages for each book on your wishlist.
  • storage — persist the most recent scan locally so the popup opens instantly.
  • host_permissions: https://www.amazon.com/* — required to read your wishlist page and the offer-listing pages for the books on it. No non-Amazon domains are accessed.

Data sharing and sale

  • We do not sell or transfer user data to third parties.
  • We do not use or transfer user data for purposes unrelated to the extension's single purpose.
  • We do not use or transfer user data to determine creditworthiness or for lending purposes.

Source code

Cheapest Read is open source. The extension code, release history, and this privacy policy are all available at github.com/jamesfishwick/amazon-used.

Changes to this policy

Changes to this policy are tracked in the repository's git history. The "Effective" date above reflects the most recent substantive update. Material changes will also be noted in the extension's CHANGELOG.
